UNIT CONVERTION

 
 
 

FORMULAS

  • Second :
    • Seconds to Minutes (s to min):
      Formula: Minutes = Seconds / 60
    • Seconds to Hours (s to h):
      Formula: Hours = Seconds / 3600
    • Seconds to Days (s to d):
      Formula: Days = Seconds / 86,400
    • Seconds to Weeks (s to wk):
      Formula: Weeks = Seconds / 604,800s
    • Seconds to Years (s to yr):
      Formula: Years = Seconds / 31,536,000
  • Minutes :
    • Minutes to Seconds (min to s):
      Formula: Seconds = Minutes * 60
    • Minutes to Hours (min to h):
      Formula: Hours = Minutes / 60
    • Minutes to Days (min to d):
      Formula: Days = Minutes / 1,440
    • Minutes to Weeks (min to wk):
      Formula: Weeks = Minutes / 10,080
    • Minutes to Years (min to yr):
      Formula: Years = Minutes / 525,600
  • Hours :
    • Hours to Seconds (h to s):
      Formula: Seconds = Hours * 3,600
    • Hours to Minutes (h to min):
      Formula: Minutes = Hours * 60
    • Hours to Days (h to d):
      Formula: Days = Hours / 24
    • Hours to Weeks (h to wk):
      Formula: Weeks = Hours / 168
    • Hours to Years (h to yr):
      Formula: Years = Hours / 8,760
